Privacy Policy

Toolrental is an offline-first app. All data you enter — rentals and related details — is stored locally on your device using the app's private storage. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There are no analytics services, no third-party trackers, and no network requests made by this app beyond Apple's own App Store and StoreKit purchase infrastructure, which handles in-app purchases directly with Apple.

If you delete the app, your data is deleted with it. We do not have access to your data and cannot retrieve it.
